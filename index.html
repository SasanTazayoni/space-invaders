Instructions

Controls

To Navigate: Use the arrow keys or numpad (left and right) or the "A" and "D" keys on the keyboard.

To Shoot: Press the spacebar on the keyboard.

Enemies

Difficulty increase and points

Clearing a wave of aliens gives 10,000 points. Every 100,000 points clear a stage, and the game's difficulty slightly increases. Currently, there are 10 stages, but more may unlock in the future as adventurers progress further - although, as of yet, no adventurer has survived long enough to see beyond stage 10. But who knows, maybe someone will break that record!
